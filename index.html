Top three activities to do at Pakistan

Karakoram and Himalayan Range

Pakistan is home to K2, the world's second-highest mountain peak, after Mount Everest.By an impressive height of 8,611 meters (28,251 feet)

The Changa Manga forest is one of the largest manmade forests in the world covers an area of around 12,423 acres and was planted to combat deforestation.
